Animal Behavior

Mid-term Paper

To investigate the reason why my dogs often act excitedly when hearing the sound of keys, I made two hypotheses and then performed an experiment by observing my pet dogs' reactions toward stimulations like the ringing of keys. At last, I concluded that dogs’ overreactions are likely because certain events represent a high possibility of going out.

Genetics Experiment

Part of my experiments results

Mfn1 (mediates mitochondria fusion) was knocked out in the Hela cell using CRISPR/Cas9. Mitochondria were stained by Mito-GFP, red stain indicates cells transfected by plasmid px330-Cherry. In this experiment, the absence of functioning Mfn1 halts mitochondria fusion, therefore the mitochondria in successfully transfected cells appear in small shapes.

The F1 generation of three different Drosophila melanogaster crosses. This experiment was designed to introduce Gal4/UAS system. GMR-Gal4 is specifically expressed in eyes. YAP is an oncogene and its mutant YAPS127A gets into the cell nucleus more easily.
